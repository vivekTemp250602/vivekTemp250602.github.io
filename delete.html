Data Deletion Request for Naam Jaap

We respect your right to privacy and control over your personal data. You can request the permanent deletion of your Naam Jaap account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Naam Jaap application on your device.
  2. Navigate to the "Profile" tab.
  3. Tap on the "Feedback & Support" option.
  4. In the form that opens, please state clearly that you wish to request account deletion.
  5. Please provide the email address associated with your Naam Jaap account for verification.

Alternatively, you can use the direct link to our support form here: Naam Jaap Support Form

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently delete the following information from our systems:

  • Your Firebase Authentication record (your login account).
  • Your Cloud Firestore user document, which includes your name, email address, photo URL, all japa counts (total, weekly, and per-mantra), your daily streak, and any badges you have earned.
  • Your uploaded profile picture from Firebase Storage.

What Data is Not Deleted?

Please note that any anonymous, aggregated usage data collected by Google Analytics for Firebase, which is not personally identifiable, may be retained for analytical purposes.

We will process your request and confirm the deletion via email, typically within 7-14 business days.

Thank you for being a part of our community.
